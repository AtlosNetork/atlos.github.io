Atlos Privacy Policy

Atlos is a mentorship platform designed to connect high school athletes with current college athletes for academic, athletic, and personal development.

Information We Collect

Atlos collects information provided by users during account creation and use of the platform. This may include name, email address, school affiliation, profile information, and messages exchanged within the app.

How Information Is Used

Information collected by Atlos is used to operate and improve the platform, facilitate mentorship connections, enable communication between users, and maintain a safe and functional user experience.

Data Sharing

Atlos does not sell user data or share personal information with third parties for advertising or tracking purposes.

Information may be shared only with service providers necessary to operate core platform functionality, such as secure messaging and notification delivery.

User Content

Messages and profile content are visible only to relevant users within the platform. Content may be reviewed by administrators for moderation, safety, or compliance with community guidelines.

Data Security

Atlos takes reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no system can guarantee complete security.

Children’s Privacy

Atlos is intended for users involved in organized athletics and mentorship. Users under the age of 18 should use the platform with parental awareness and consent.
