Atlos Support

Atlos is a mentorship platform that connects high school athletes with current college athletes to support academic, athletic, and personal development.

If you need help using the platform, have questions about features, or need to report an issue, please contact our support team.

Safety and Community Standards

Atlos is committed to maintaining a respectful and safe environment for all users.

Harassment, inappropriate content, or misuse of the platform is not tolerated. Reports of violations may be reviewed and acted upon by administrators.
