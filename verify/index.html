Credential Verification

Atlos provides verified mentor credentials to recognize collegiate athletes who have demonstrated meaningful mentorship within the platform.

How a mentor earns an Atlos credential

Atlos mentor credentials are awarded to mentors who actively participate in the platform through consistent, high-quality mentorship. Certification is granted only after specific activity and engagement criteria are met, including sustained communication, completed mentorship interactions, and strong standing within the platform.

Why public verification exists

Public verification ensures that each Atlos credential is real, earned, and tied to a specific individual. Every certified mentor receives a unique credential ID and verification URL that can be shared on LinkedIn or a resume so others can confirm its authenticity.

Verify a credential

If you have a credential link or credential ID, use the full verification URL provided with the certificate to view that mentor’s credential record.
